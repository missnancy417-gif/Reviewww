Exponential and Logarithmic Function 🍎
📚 Core Knowledge (Properties of Logarithms)
Let \(a > 0\), \(a ≠ 1\) and \(x, y > 0\).

(a) \(\log_a a^x = x\)
(b) \(\log_a 1 = 0\)
(c) \(\log_a a = 1\)
(d) Product Rule: \(\log_a xy = \log_a x + \log_a y\)
(e) Quotient Rule: \(\log_a (x/y) = \log_a x − \log_a y\)
(f) Power Rule: \(\log_a x^k = k \log_a x\) (k is any real number)
